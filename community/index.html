The Ubuntu Community

We are an international community with a mission to bring free software to the widest possible audience. Our name “Ubuntu” means “humanity towards others”. Join us and share in our mission to bring free software to the entire world!

We believe that technology should empower people, not restrict them. By embracing open-source principles, we create tools that anyone can use, modify, and share. Our community thrives on collaboration, innovation, and the shared goal of making digital freedom a reality for everyone.

Our mission goes beyond just providing free software; it is about creating an inclusive environment where everyone, regardless of background or skill level, can contribute to and benefit from open-source projects. We organize community events, online discussions, and mentorship programs to ensure that knowledge and resources are accessible to all. By fostering collaboration across continents and cultures, we enable people to learn from each other, develop new skills, and work together on projects that have real-world impact.
